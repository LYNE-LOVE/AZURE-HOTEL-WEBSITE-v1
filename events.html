Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about attending our events

What is your refund policy?

+

Full refunds are available up to 14 days before the event. Between 7-14 days, a 50% refund is provided. Tickets are non-refundable within 7 days of the event. However, tickets are transferable to another person.

What is the dress code?

+

Dress codes vary by event. The Annual Gala requires black tie attire. Tennis Championship is sports casual. Culinary Festival is smart casual. Wedding Expo has no specific dress code. Specific requirements are listed on each event ticket.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

+

Yes! All Azure Horizon event spaces are fully wheelchair accessible with ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms. We also offer reserved accessible parking and seating. Please indicate accessibility needs when registering so we can ensure your comfort.

Can I book accommodation at the hotel?

+

Absolutely! Event attendees receive 20% off room rates. Use the event code provided in your confirmation email when booking. Rooms can be reserved up to 3 months in advance and must be booked at least 48 hours before the event.

Are children allowed at events?

+

It depends on the event. The Culinary Festival and Wedding Expo are family-friendly. The Annual Gala is adults-only (21+). Tennis Championship allows children with adult supervision. Age restrictions are clearly marked on each event listing.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. Payment is processed securely through our encrypted system. Corporate accounts and wire transfers are available for group bookings.
